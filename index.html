Arriving at WOB

·        Where?  You are coming to YMCA Camp Letts in Edgewater, Maryland! 

·        When? The recommended arrival time is between 4 and 5:30 pm on Friday, March 28th. 

·        Important: Be sure to follow the 15 MPH speed limit on camp property

·        Arrival: Upon arrival park at the Dining Hall and come to the registration desk inside to check in and sign up for workshops and volunteering opportunities.  Then, move your things to your cabin, and after that, please move your car to parking at the Athletic Field to make room for others. 

What to Bring

Please review the Packing List below and note that there is no bedding provided at camp--please bring a sleeping bag or other bedding, as that is not provided by Camp Letts.  

·         A flexible and generous attitude!

·         Bedding - You must bring a sleeping bag OR a blanket and sheets

·         Pillow and pillow case

·         Optional: Foam padding to make your mattress more comfortable

·         Optional: eye shade and ear plugs (we are sharing bunk rooms!)

·         Towel for bathing

·         Toiletries (toothbrush, toothpaste, soap, shampoo etc.)

·         Clothes for all weather – it is windy on the waterfront!

·         Closed toe and tennis shoes or sneakers for ziplining and team development courses.  

·         Water shoes, sandals or sneakers that can get wet for canoeing

·         Raincoat or poncho

·         Water bottle

·         Sun screen

·         Flashlight/lantern/headlamp

·         Camp Chair (there is not a lot of seating available outside of the Dining Hall)

·         Snacks

·         Games

·         Book

·         A reusable coffee mug (there will be compostable mugs in the dining hall, but you can help us reduce waste by bringing your own)

·         Bikes/scooters for children

·         Anything you need for the talent show

·         Yoga Mat or towel if you’d like to participate in the Yoga or Pilates workshops.  

·         Supplies for workshops you might be offering

·         Knitting/Crocheting/Needlecrafts for the Needlecrafts Meetup
